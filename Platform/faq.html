FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find Resources?

Use the search functionality provided or you can browse all courses to find the one you're looking for.


You can search by the name of the Topic, a Subtopic, by Source, or by Instructor's Name.

How do I start learning from a Resource?

After you click on a given link to a resource, you'll be redirected to the resource page where you'll find every possible detail about the resource.


Then to get started just click "Get Started" and you'll be taken to the Resource Home Page on the host website.

Why should I create an Account?

It is recommended to create an Account to submit a Resource Link, to get latest resources first and to be able to rate and review resources.

How can I create an Account?

Use the Login option given on the top of every page to Create an Account.


Fill in your details and an Account with the entered credentials will be created.

How do I share my progress?

After you create an Account we'll be keeping a track of what courses you enroll in and what tests and quizzes you take. You can directly share your progress through the Social Media links provided.

Why ByteCode?

We are an ever-growing community of programmers, designers and hackers. You'll be recommended courses and quizzes based on your usage and performances making your learning experience awesome and complete.


Besides we know what people go through when they are flooded with options in the internet era, we'll help you to be more focused and productive.

About us

One of the biggest challenges in the field of Computer Sciences is that people often find themselves confused on what to learn and even if they decide on one thing, there are zillions of resources. We want to help people learn from the best sources available, organizing them under one roof.
