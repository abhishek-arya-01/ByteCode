About Us

Created with a goal to help students achieve maximum results and to help them find the true programmming language or concept they love, also allowing them to browse through hundreds of resources and weblinks to allow maximum exposure to various :

Web Resources (Free as well as Paid), Online open-source projects, Competitions and Quizzes, enabling them to track and sharing progress, collaborate and develop as a community.

In this era of rapidly increasing technologies in the field of Computer Science, it is very hard for people to choose one programming language or even operating system for themselves,

Student are often confused nowadays on what to study. We hereby combine the learning fun of Computers and Academics and try to provide you with resources used by other people to learn.

The User Review and Course/Tutorial Rating feature enables people to choose among millions of links and web resources, based on other people's opinions and what they learnt from it.

We cache links and resources all over the internet and provide you with a window through which you can learn all of them at one place, no randomly surfing the internet which ends up wasting your time eventually.

Spend that time on actually learning the topic, practicing again and again and enjoy...!
